Mamma's Recipe for Best Brownies :)

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Dark Chocolate
  • ½ Cup Butter
  • 1½ cup granulated Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • ¼tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Vanilla Essence
  • ½ Cup All purpose flour
  • ¼ Cup Coco Powder

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a utensil.
  2. Add dark chocolate in a glass bowl and place it on top of the utensil.
  3. Add butter to the melting chooclate.
  4. In another bowl add granulated sugar and add eggs.
  5. Add 1 egg at a time and mix after each addition.
  6. Add vanilla essence
  7. Add the chocolate butter mixture and mix well.
  8. Add all purpose flour and coco powder and sift it together.
  9. Gently fold them into the batter.
  10. Your batter is ready.

Baking:

  • Baking tray.
  • Butter paper.
  • Oil

For Baking follow these instructions:

  1. Grease the butter paper with oil.
  2. Map the butter paper on a baking tray.
  3. Pour the batter into the tray.
  4. Tap the tray gently so that the mixture is evenly spread in the tray.
  5. Place the tray in the Oven as per the degrees of requirement menitoned below.

Note:

Bake the mixture for 30mins
350ºF/180ºC
In the preheated oven.

Presentation:

Cut the brownie in even square pieces with a clean knife each time dipped in a luke warm water and cleaned with a paper towel

Yes!
The perfect Fudgy Brownies are ready.
